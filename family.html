The Family

Having began as a small, family-owned business over a century ago, Pacific Trails Resort considers every employee and visitor as part of its family. We owe all our success to the hardworking individuals that shape this marvel of nature and the lovely patrons that we're fortunate enough to host. As such, the safety and enjoyment of our visitors is paramount. Whether you're an annual guest or this is your first time visiting, we here at Pacific Trails Resort would like to say welcome home!

The Resort

The luster of California's natural beauty is on full display at Pacific Trails Resort. Every amenity you could possibly desire can be found here. From the dazzling blues and aquamarines of the Pacific Ocean to the deep greens and browns of mother Earth, we're positive that our resort will invoke or rekindle your love of the great outdoors.

What to expect?

Pacific Trails Resort is home to over 5 miles of secluded hiking trails, beaches, and forests. Guests can enjoy ocean kayaking, guided or solo hiking, walking on the beaches, and participating in our world-class birdwatching. Pacific Trails Resort sports an extremely high amount of biodiversity so even if birds aren't your thing, there will be plenty of interesting plants and animals to look at!

Our Purpose

Pacific Trails Resort was originally established as an outpost for wilderness monitoring and research. The decision to open access to the public comes from our deeply ingrained philosophy that humans should have access to all of nature's splendors. In addition, we have another priority — preservation. We ask that all visitors respect these lands as if it were a national preserve. If you feel up to it during your stay, please join us for a weekly wilderness cleanup for a chance to win prizes!
